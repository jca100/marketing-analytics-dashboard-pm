Retrospective & Business Impact

What Went Well

  • Early alignment on KPIs reduced confusion and prevented rework later in the project
  • Regular stakeholder check-ins kept expectations clear and helped resolve blockers quickly
  • Delivering an MVP enabled an on-time launch and created a foundation for iteration

What Didn’t Go Well

  • Some metric definitions required additional clarification later than expected
  • The tight timeline limited time for exploring advanced dashboard features
  • Feedback cycles occasionally slowed due to competing stakeholder priorities

What I’d Do Differently

  • Schedule a dedicated KPI alignment session earlier to finalize definitions faster
  • Run lightweight usability testing before stakeholder review to improve clarity
  • Build additional buffer time for iteration and last-minute feedback

Business Impact Summary

The internal marketing analytics dashboard provided stakeholders with a centralized, reliable view of campaign performance, improving visibility and enabling faster decision-making. Consolidating key metrics reduced time spent on manual reporting and helped align the team around consistent KPI definitions.

By prioritizing a focused MVP under tight constraints, the project delivered value quickly while setting the foundation for future enhancements based on stakeholder feedback and adoption trends.

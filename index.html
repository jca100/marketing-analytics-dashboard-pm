Internal Marketing Analytics Dashboard

Role: Project Manager
Timeline: Two months
Project Type: Internal analytics platform launch

Problem Statement

The marketing team relies on multiple disconnected reporting tools to evaluate campaign performance, resulting in fragmented data, inconsistent metrics, and time-consuming manual reporting. Stakeholders lack a single source of truth, which slows decision-making and limits the teamâ€™s ability to optimize campaigns efficiently.

This project focused on planning and launching an internal marketing analytics dashboard that consolidates key performance metrics into one centralized view, improving visibility, alignment, and decision-making across the marketing organization.

Project Goals

  • Provide a single source of truth for campaign performance
  • Reduce time spent on manual and ad-hoc reporting
  • Improve stakeholder alignment through consistent KPI definitions

Success Metrics

  • Dashboard adoption rate among marketing stakeholders
  • Reduction in time spent preparing weekly or monthly reports
  • Positive qualitative feedback from marketing leadership

Stakeholders & Users

  • Marketing Leadership: Executive visibility and performance oversight
  • Campaign Managers: Ongoing campaign optimization and analysis
  • Marketing Analyst: Data validation and reporting support

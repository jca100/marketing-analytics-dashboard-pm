Risks & Decision Log

Risk Register

Risk Impact Mitigation Strategy
Misalignment on KPI definitions across stakeholders High Facilitate early alignment sessions and document KPI definitions prior to dashboard build
Scope creep from additional metric requests High Define MVP scope clearly and defer non-essential requests to a post-launch backlog
Data inconsistencies or delays in refresh cadence Medium Agree on refresh schedule early and validate data sources during QA
Low dashboard adoption after launch Medium Incorporate stakeholder feedback early and provide light training documentation
Tight timeline limits iteration Medium Prioritize core KPIs and conduct early reviews to reduce rework

Decision Log

Decision Options Considered Final Choice Rationale
Scope of initial dashboard Full-featured dashboard vs. MVP MVP dashboard Ensured on-time delivery while still meeting stakeholder needs
Number of KPIs included Comprehensive set vs. core KPIs Core KPIs only Improved clarity and avoided information overload
Data refresh cadence Real-time vs. scheduled refresh Scheduled refresh Reduced complexity while supporting decision-making needs
Dashboard design approach Custom visuals vs. standardized layout Standardized layout Improved usability and reduced build time

Tracking risks and documenting key decisions throughout the project helped maintain transparency, manage trade-offs, and ensure alignment with stakeholder priorities under a tight delivery timeline.
